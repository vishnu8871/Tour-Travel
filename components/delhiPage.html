Delhi

The most places to visit near Delhi are Red Fort, India Gate, Lotus Temple. Delhiites often wear a mix of traditional and Western clothing such as saris, salwar kameez. Hindi is the official language of Delhi and is widely spoken by the majority of the population. Urdu and Punjabi are spoken by significant sections of the population, particularly in Old Delhi and areas with a concentration of Punjabi communities. Delhi is a culinary haven with a diverse range of restaurants offering everything from traditional Indian cuisine to international flavors.
