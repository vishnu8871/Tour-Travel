Kerala

The most places to visit near Kerala are Munnar, Alleppey, Kovalam. Women and Men often wear a mix of traditional attire. Malayalam is the official language of Kerala and is widely spoken by the majority of the population. Hindi and Tamil are spoken by significant sections of the population. Kerala is known for its culinary tradition and can find a variety of restaurants offering delicious local and international cuisine. Here are some famous and popular restaurants in Kerala such as Traditional Kerala Restaurants, Multi - cuisine restaurants, Street food stalls many more..... Kerala offers a wide range of accommodation options to suit various budgets and preferences. The city has something for everyone like luxury resorts like The Leela Kovalam, KumaraKom Lake Resort and Boutique Hotels like The Postcard Mandalay Hall in Fort Kochi, Xandari Harbour in Kochi. When visiting Kerala, it's important to be aware of local customs and traditions to ensure a respectful and enjoyable experience like Respect Local Customs, Appropriate clothing, Respect the Environment, Try local Cuisine, Negotiate Fairly, Be Polite and Courteous and dont's be like Don't Disrespect Religious Symbols, Disrespecting Customs, Littering, Disrupt Wildlife, Ignore Local advice.
